Adventure Activities & Zipline Croatia

Expert-led adrenaline experiences with professional safety equipment at every station

12.5 m High Swing

Soar through the Istrian forest canopy on one of Croatia's tallest adventure swings. After a brief climb to the launch platform, you'll swing in a wide arc 12.5 metres above the ground — a breathtaking rush with panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Trained staff fit you with a full harness before every ride.

12.5 m drop

Human Catapult

Experience one of Europe's most extreme park attractions: launch from zero to 100 km/h in just one second. The Human Catapult uses a precision-engineered bungee system with redundant safety lines and pre-ride health checks. Ideal for adventurous adults and teenagers seeking a genuine G-force thrill in Istria.

0 → 100 km/h in 1 s

Ziplines

Fly up to 120 metres along lines reaching 20 metres above the forest floor — a highlight for anyone searching for zipline Croatia experiences. The blue and black high-ropes routes include a 113 m zipline between platforms, while the standalone 120 m line offers an extended flight through the treetops with instructor supervision throughout.

Up to 120 m long

Climbing Wall

Test your strength, balance, and technique on our outdoor climbing wall. Routes vary in difficulty so beginners can learn basic holds while experienced climbers push their limits. All participants receive a harness and helmet, and staff provide a safety briefing before the first ascent.

All skill levels

Quick Jump

The Quick Jump attraction lets visitors safely experience a controlled free fall from 20 metres. Participants step off a platform wearing professional safety equipment while a descender system manages the descent speed. Instructor guidance and a pre-jump briefing make this suitable for adventurous teenagers and adults seeking an adrenaline activity in Istria.

20 m controlled free fall

High Ropes Course Croatia

Three aerial routes designed for different ages and confidence levels

Yellow Route

2 m high — Our family-friendly introduction to aerial adventure. Balance beams, rope bridges, and low platforms build confidence in children and first-timers. A great starting point for things to do in Istria with kids.

Blue Route

6 m high — Intermediate challenge with longer crossings and an integrated 113 m zipline between the first and last platform. Popular with teens and adults who want more height without the full black-route intensity.

Black Route

10 m high — The ultimate test for experienced adventurers. Demanding obstacles at maximum height reward those who conquer every element. Continuous harness connection keeps you safe throughout.

Glavani Park is designed for the whole family. Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from first-time visitors planning their Istria adventure

Is Glavani Park suitable for children?

Yes. The 2-metre yellow high-ropes route is designed specifically for younger children and families. Several ground-level activities are also suitable for kids, while taller attractions like the Human Catapult and Quick Jump have minimum age and height requirements. Contact us before visiting if you're unsure which activities fit your group.

How long does a visit take?

Most visitors spend 3–4 hours enjoying the park. If you plan to complete multiple high-ropes routes plus ziplines and the swing, arrive before midday. Last entry is at 3 PM, and the park closes at 5 PM.

Do I need a reservation?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we strongly recommend calling in advance — especially for groups, weekends, and peak summer season. Phone +385 91 896 4525 (English) or +385 98 224 314 (Croatian) to check availability.

Is parking available?

Yes, free on-site parking is available for cars, minibuses, and coaches. The car park is a short walk from the ticket area and main attractions.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and closed-toe sports shoes (trainers or hiking shoes). Avoid sandals, flip-flops, and loose jewellery. In summer, bring sunscreen and water — we also sell drinks and ice cream on site.

Is it safe?

Safety is our top priority. All participants receive professional harnesses, helmets, and a briefing before each activity. Equipment is inspected daily, routes are supervised by trained instructors, and continuous belay systems keep you connected at height on the high-ropes courses.
