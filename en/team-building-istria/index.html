Team Building Program

Book Tickets Call to Book

When you hear team building, you think of activity, socialising and fun — then ideas about bringing colleagues closer and lifting workplace morale.

Barban is where some of the best team building programmes in Istria are organised for companies large and small — from 10 to more than 200 people.

As Croatia's largest adrenaline park we offer games you won't find anywhere else: the tallest outdoor artificial climbing wall, the horizontal Human Catapult, and an 11 m high swing that leaves nobody indifferent. We organise hours of team bonding and fun — with food and drink an essential part of every programme.

What a team building day for 30 people looks like: After arriving at Glavani Park and a short introduction, the group splits in two — 15 to the 11 m high swing, 15 to the yellow, blue and black high-ropes routes.

When the first group finishes the swing they swap to climbing; when the second group completes the swing (after roughly two hours on the courses), everyone gathers for food and drinks, followed by a friendly competition on the 12 m climbing wall and a tug-of-war. Teams are split the same way as at the start.

Two people climb at once on the 12 m wall; each person climbs twice (once on each side) and times are added. The faster climber advances. To keep it fair, the target height rises towards the final, where only a climb to the top counts.

While the wall competition is individual, tug-of-war is pure teamwork. After the winner is crowned, the team relaxes with conversation, music, drinks and table tennis.

For larger groups — 50, 100 or 200 — we organise programmes with partners Istra Adventure and Ranch Barba Tone: horse riding, quad and buggy tours, kayaking, Escape Castle in Savičenta, hiking and cycling around Žminj.

Contact us with confidence and leave the organisation of your day or weekend to us. Options include Glavani Park, horse riding, quad/buggy/kayak, food at konoba Vorichi, Escape Castle, cycling, walking and exploring Istria, picnics in nature, and vintage games.

Call +385 91 896 4525 to plan your team building event. The park is open daily 9 AM–5 PM (last entry 3 PM).

Frequently asked questions

What group sizes can Glavani Park accommodate?

The park regularly hosts groups from 10 to 200+ participants. Larger corporate events may require staggered scheduling — call +385 91 896 4525 to discuss your headcount and preferred date.

Are team building packages available in English?

Yes. English-speaking instructors lead briefings and provide on-course support. Croatian-language coordination is also available for local companies.

Can we combine team building with catering?

Refreshments and ice cream are available on site. For full catering or reserved celebration areas, discuss options when booking your corporate package in advance.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least two to four weeks ahead for groups, and earlier for peak summer dates. The park is open daily 9 AM–5 PM; last entry is 3 PM.
