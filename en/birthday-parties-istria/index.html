Birthdays in Glavani Park

Book Online Call to Book

Surprise your child with a celebration they won't forget.

A birthday at Glavani Park is an outdoor celebration where children socialise, meet the park's friendly animals, play fun games, get to know each other better and retell everything with enthusiasm. Give them an unforgettable experience.

Natural surroundings in the oak forest · 90% of games made from wood · Dogs and cats on site · Games tailored to the birthday child's age.

Children learn that winning isn't everything — helping each other matters. They discover what teamwork means, and parents: in our experience, you'll have just as much fun.

Party packages include priority access to attractions, a reserved area for cake and gifts, and staff who understand the flow of a celebration day. Younger groups enjoy the yellow training route and climbing wall; teens add ziplines, the high swing, and for the boldest, the Human Catapult.

Most birthday groups spend three to four hours at the park: safety briefing, activities on the courses and ziplines, then cake in the shaded picnic area. Soft drinks and ice cream are available on site; many families bring their own birthday cake.

The park is open daily 9 AM–5 PM with last entry at 3 PM. Arrive by 9:30 AM for a full morning, or before 1 PM for an afternoon celebration.

Call +385 91 896 4525 or +385 98 224 314 to reserve your date, guest count, and ages. Advance booking is essential for birthday parties.

Frequently asked questions

What age is best for a Glavani Park birthday party?

Parties work well from around age 6 upward. The yellow high-ropes route suits younger children; teens enjoy ziplines and taller courses. Call +385 91 896 4525 to discuss the best activities for your group's ages.

Can we bring our own cake and decorations?

Yes. Party packages include a dedicated celebration area. You are welcome to bring cake, snacks, and modest decorations. Please avoid confetti or items that could litter the forest.

How many guests can attend a birthday party?

Group sizes vary. Contact the park with your expected headcount so we can arrange priority access and adequate instructor coverage.

How do I book a birthday party?

Birthday parties must be arranged by phone — call +385 91 896 4525 with your preferred date, guest count, and ages.
