Book

Fill in the form and submit your booking — we aim to email you to confirm within 24 hours. Check your spam or junk folder if you do not see our email.

Payment: card and cash accepted at the park. On site: refreshments and ice cream available.

Children's prices (under 10 years old): training route + 2 games (all games except catapult) €20 · whole park without catapult €40 · whole park incl. catapult €60 per child. Select adults and children in the form.

Booking policy: From July through the end of September, walk-ins are welcome during opening hours (9 AM–5 PM, last entry 3 PM), subject to availability — call ahead to avoid disappointment, as we may not be able to accommodate walk-ins when we have a large group booking. From the end of September until the start of July, all visits require advance booking; walk-ins are not accepted.
