FAQ

Quick answers for visitors planning a day at Glavani Park.

What are your opening hours?

Open daily 9 AM–5 PM. Last entry is 3 PM — allow three to four hours for a full visit.

Do I need to book ahead?

Walk-ins are welcome, but booking ahead is recommended on weekends and in July and August. Fill in the online form and we'll confirm via SMS or WhatsApp as soon as possible.

How do I book online?

Choose a package on our Book page, pick a date, and send your details via WhatsApp or SMS. Within 48 hours of your visit date, please call to book instead.

How far is Glavani Park from Pula?

About 30 minutes by car from Pula, 45 from Rovinj, and 10 from Vodnjan. Free parking at Glavani 10, 52207 Barban.

What ages can take part?

The yellow training route suits younger children with adult supervision. Taller attractions such as the Human Catapult and Quick Jump have minimum age and height limits — call ahead if unsure.

What should we wear?

Closed-toe sports shoes, comfortable fitted clothing, no loose jewellery. Tie back long hair and bring sunscreen and water in summer.

Is the equipment safe?

Yes. Harnesses, helmets, and connections are CE-certified for adventure parks and checked daily before opening. Instructors brief every participant.

Is there food on site?

Coffee, soft drinks, and ice cream are available at a shaded picnic area. You may bring snacks; there is no full restaurant on site.

Can we book for a large group?

Online booking is for up to 6 people. For groups of 7 or more — corporate events, schools, or birthdays — call +385 91 896 4525 to arrange your visit.

Ready to visit? Book online for up to 6 people, or call for larger groups.
