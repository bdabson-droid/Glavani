Our Activities

Nine signature outdoor attractions — adventure park, zipline courses and adrenaline rides in Istria

Glavani Park near Barban covers 1.5 hectares of oak forest with nine instructor-led attractions — from the family yellow training route and 120 m ziplines to the Human Catapult, Devil's Causeway Course, Aerotrim, and 12.5 m high swing. Open daily 9 AM–5 PM, about 30 minutes from Pula.
